Quick Settlement Name Generator
Use the oracle provided here. The English meaning of each Japanese word is provided so that you can combine the words for the settlement in either language. (i.e. the town Washioka could also be called Eagle Hill).

Feel free to embellish the names as it can lead to more world building.

For example, if you roll and get 18 and 52 to get Washiasase or Eagle's
Ford, you might decide to name the settlement Ford of the Walking Eagle.
I thought of adding "walking" because why would an eagle need to ford a
river? Next I start developing a Firelander myth of a great eagle yokai that
wanted to learn what it meant to be human and so gave up their wings for
a year and a day. On the day that the eagle was to get back its wings, the
river flooded and the eagle jumped in to save the person it had fallen in love
with during the last year. The person survived, but the eagle yokai did not.
Now the people living in the Ford of the Walking Eagle honor its sacrifice
each year in the spring.
